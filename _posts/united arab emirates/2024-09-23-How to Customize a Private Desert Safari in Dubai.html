What is a Private Desert Safari?

A private desert safari in Dubai is a luxurious and customizable adventure designed for those who seek an exclusive and personalized experience. Unlike group safaris, a private desert safari allows you to tailor every aspect of your journey, from the type of vehicle for dune bashing to the activities and dining options you want to include. Whether you want a thrilling ride through the dunes or a peaceful camel trek, the experience can be fully tailored to your preferences.

How to Customize Your Private Desert Safari

Customizing your private desert safari is easy, and there are several ways to make it a truly personalized experience:

  1. Choose Your Preferred Vehicle: Depending on your preference for adventure or comfort, you can select from various vehicles such as a luxury SUV, a classic Land Rover, or even a quad bike for more extreme experiences.
  2. Pick Your Activities: You can add activities like dune bashing, sandboarding, falconry shows, camel riding, or even hot air balloon rides. Many safari operators offer a range of options that you can mix and match to create the perfect itinerary.
  3. Select Your Dining Experience: Many luxury desert safaris offer private dining under the stars, featuring gourmet meals prepared on-site. You can opt for traditional Bedouin cuisine or choose a more modern menu to suit your taste. Some operators even offer the option to enjoy your meal in a secluded desert camp with exclusive service.
  4. Determine the Duration: While standard safaris last around 4-6 hours, you can choose extended options such as full-day tours or overnight experiences, where you can sleep in a luxury tent and wake up to the sunrise over the dunes.
  5. Arrange for VIP Services: Many operators offer VIP packages that include private transfers, personal guides, and exclusive access to certain areas of the desert for a more intimate and unique experience.

Best Private Desert Safari Operators in Dubai

Here are some of the top operators that offer private desert safari experiences in Dubai:

  • Platinum Heritage: Known for offering eco-friendly and luxury desert safaris, Platinum Heritage allows guests to experience the desert in style. Their private tours include activities like wildlife drives and cultural experiences, all while ensuring minimal environmental impact.
  • Arabian Adventures: One of the most established safari operators in Dubai, Arabian Adventures offers bespoke private safaris that can include a wide range of activities such as dune bashing, stargazing, and private dining.
  • Royal Shaheen Events: Specializing in falconry experiences, Royal Shaheen offers a highly customizable safari where guests can learn about the tradition of falconry while enjoying a private desert adventure.

Tips for a Perfect Private Desert Safari Experience

To ensure that your private desert safari in Dubai is everything you want it to be, here are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Book During Cooler Months: The best time for a desert safari in Dubai is between October and March, when the temperatures are more comfortable for outdoor activities.
  • Plan for Sunrise or Sunset: Sunrise and sunset safaris offer the most spectacular views of the desert and are also the best times for photography. Consider scheduling your safari during these times for an unforgettable experience.
  • Wear Comfortable Clothing: Light, breathable fabrics are ideal for desert adventures, and itâ€™s also recommended to bring a light jacket for the cooler evening temperatures.
  • Communicate Your Preferences Early: If you have specific requests, such as dietary preferences or particular activities, itâ€™s important to communicate these to the operator when booking so they can make all the necessary arrangements in advance.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a private desert safari in Dubai?

A private desert safari in Dubai is an exclusive experience where you and your group enjoy a tailored adventure in the desert, including activities like dune bashing, camel rides, and private dining.

How can I customize my desert safari experience?

You can customize your desert safari by selecting specific activities such as falconry, sandboarding, and luxury dining options. You can also choose the duration and the type of vehicle used for dune bashing.

What are the best times for a desert safari?

The best times for a desert safari are early in the morning or during the evening when temperatures are cooler, and you can witness either the sunrise or sunset over the dunes.
